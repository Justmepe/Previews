Our Equipment

Cutting-Edge
Technology

We invest continuously in the most advanced diagnostic and surgical equipment available in East Africa.

🔆
Alcon WaveLight EX500 LASIK Laser

One of the fastest excimer lasers in the world — 500Hz repetition rate for precise, comfortable LASIK treatments in under 10 seconds per eye.

FDA Approved · Available at WLEH
📡
Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT)

High-resolution retinal cross-sectional imaging — detects glaucoma, macular degeneration, and diabetic changes years before symptoms appear.

Zeiss Cirrus · Available at WLEH
🗺️
Corneal Topography & Wavefront

3D corneal mapping for custom LASIK planning. Detects keratoconus before surgery to ensure every patient's long-term safety.

Pentacam · Available at WLEH
📸
Digital Retinal Photography

Wide-field fundus imaging capturing the full retina for longitudinal tracking and telemedicine consultations with international specialists.

Optos Daytona · Available at WLEH
